Abstract

            

Kennesaw State University (KSU) has much artwork and landmarks throughout the Kennesaw and Marietta Campus. Most students may not know what artwork they may pass by or what the story behind it might be. With our app, art will be located and documented; it will allow students and guests to learn about their environment. Allowing clients to request landmarks will keep the app updated and allow a high level of interaction.
